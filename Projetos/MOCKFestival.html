WORTEN MOCK FEST - 3D Mockup

July / August 2025

For the WORTEN MOCK FEST, I was responsible for the 3D visualization of the main stage and festival area, collaborating closely with the producer to bring his vision to life. I created a simplified 3D construction of the venue to help visualize the future event, making adjustments to the different areas whenever necessary. The project included detailed renders, layout planning, a 360º interactive experience to facilitate visualization.

WORTEN MOCK FEST Official Website

Below you can explore the 360º virtual tour of the festival area. The three dots in the bottom left corner allow you to directly choose a location. The "Top View" button lets you see the avenue and explore the exterior area in front of Cinema São Jorge. Use your mouse or touch to navigate!

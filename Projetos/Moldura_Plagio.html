Mona Lisa Frame - "PLÁGIO AO VIVO"

December 1, 2024

The project 'PLÁGIO AO VIVO' was a live show by Mafalda Creative and Mariana Bossy. I designed a large Renaissance-style 3D frame inspired by the Mona Lisa's, which was printed in pieces at a total size of 3m x 4m.

MAFALDA CREATIVE E MARIANA BOSSY - PLÁGIO AO VIVO

During the process, I handled the STL file preparation to ensure the frame was ready for large-scale 3D printing.

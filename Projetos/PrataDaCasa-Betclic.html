PODCAST "Prata da Casa" - Betclic

March 27, 2025

"Prata da Casa" is a weekly podcast produced by KILT in partnership with Betclic, bringing together three comedian: André Pinheiro, Vítor Sá, and Luís Franco-Bastos, to talk about sports and much more. Every Wednesday, a new episode is released.

For this project, I was responsible for creating a digital 3D version of the stage together with the art director, Marina Faria, for approval by the brand and the comedians. The set design went through several changes during pre-production until reaching the final result.

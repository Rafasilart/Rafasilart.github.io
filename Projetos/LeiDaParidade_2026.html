Lei da Paridade 2026 - Mockup 3D

May 25, 2026

For the Lei da Paridade 2026 live event in Lisbon (Teatro Tivoli BBVA) and Porto (Teatro Sá Da Bandeira), I collaborated with the production company to design and assemble the 3D stage scenery.

About the event: In this new live show, Adriana, Leonor, and Maria step outside the Expresso newsroom to debate a simple yet controversial question: who is afraid of equality? Through witty banter and firm convictions, they tackle feminism, culture, and daily life in a sharp conversation about the state of the world. You can find more details on the official Lei da Paridade

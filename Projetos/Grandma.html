Grandma - Character from 3D Animation "HAKO"

July 17, 2024

This character is the grandmother of the protagonist MEI who appears in the middle of the animation. She plays a maternal role, as well as a bridge between the protagonist's past and present.

Animation Synopsis:

The story explores the relationship between trauma and the individual, as well as the acceptance of the past and the awareness of dreams and imagination. Through visual metaphors and symbolism, the narrative reveals how Mei confronts her repressed emotions and discovers that, in the world, no one is truly alone.

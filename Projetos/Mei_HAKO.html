MEI - Character from 3D Animation "HAKO"

July 17, 2024

This character is part of an animation created for the final project of the Multimedia degree. The project consisted of a short 3D animated film named 'HAKO.'

The character, Mei, is a young Japanese woman seeking to uncover the truth about the box that haunts her and the meaning of her dreams.

Animation Synopsis:

The story explores the relationship between trauma and the individual, as well as the acceptance of the past and the awareness of dreams and imagination. Through visual metaphors and symbolism, the narrative reveals how Mei confronts her repressed emotions and discovers that, in the world, no one is truly alone.

Stylized Retro Room - (Art Deco Modernism)

February 15, 2024

While playing Deathloop, I was captivated by the game’s artistic direction, which draws heavily from the modernist art deco style and uses vibrant oranges. In this project, I created a room inspired by Deathloop’s abstract elements, while also incorporating influences from We Happy Few, which shares a similar aesthetic. I crafted most of the assets myself, blending them into a cohesive scene, with a few additional details—like vases and plants—sourced from elsewhere to enhance the overall look.

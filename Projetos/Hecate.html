Hecate - Hades II

January 2026

A 3D character model of Hecate from Hades II, completed in approximately 2 weeks. ZBrush was used for sculpting the base body, while Blender handled accessories, clothing, materials, and lighting. Substance Painter was utilized for detailed texture refinement.

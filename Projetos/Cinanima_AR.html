Cinanima Festival - AR

January 25, 2024

This project, part of the Multimedia degree curriculum, focuses on developing an augmented reality (AR) application for two Cinanima posters. The aim is to enhance user interaction by bringing elements from a parallel world into reality. In the 1989 poster, a character trapped within emerges into our world with user assistance, searching for lost ribbons to return home. The character collects a ribbon, serving as a trophy, and departs, leaving behind the first "prize." In the 2022 poster, users interact with a lamp associated with the Aladdin story, causing smoke to animate the ribbons. This smoke eventually reveals a second "prize." Both prizes contain information about past Cinanima festival winners, highlighting the wisdom of the past.

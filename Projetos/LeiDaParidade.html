Lei da Paridade - Mockup 3D

February 15, 2025

In this project, I collaborated with the event producer from KILT to create a 3D visualization of the stage, including all design elements, for client and artist approval. At the end of this page, you can find photographs from the final event, which took place at the Theater Maria Matos.

Lei da Paridade Event

At the end, I also provide a PDF with all the measurements and necessary information, making it easier for clients and artists to understand the project and helping to translate the ideas from the producer and designer into a clear visual format.

  • About Me

I was born on July 15, 2003, in Amarante, Porto, Portugal. Although I studied sciences, my passion for multimedia led me to enroll in a multimedia course at ESMAD (Escola Superior de Media Artes e Design do Politécnico do Porto). There, I learned a variety of tools, with a particular fondness for 3D work. I primarily use Blender and Photoshop for my personal projects, although I have also had lessons in Maya.

 

In my free time, I enjoy creating scenes and animating characters in Blender. I am also deeply interested in the world of games and music; I play the clarinet and have completed the 5th grade in music at a conservatory.
